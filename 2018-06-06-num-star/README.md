# NumStar

Given a decimal string, print them in stars pattern. Put some space between each printed digit.

## Patterns

## 1

```
*
*
*
*
*
```

## 2

```
 *** 
*   *
  ** 
 *   
*****
```

## 3

```
 *** 
*   *
   * 
*   *
 *** 
```

## 4

```
   * 
  ** 
 * * 
*****
   * 
```

## 5

```
*****
*    
**** 
    *
**** 
```

## 6

```
 *** 
*    
**** 
*   *
 *** 
```

## 7

```
*****
    *
   * 
  *  
 *   
```

## 8

```
 *** 
*   *
 *** 
*   *
 *** 
```

## 9

```
 *** 
*   *
 ****
    *
 *** 
```

## 0

```
 *** 
*   *
*   *
*   *
 *** 
```

## Example

1234567890

```
*   ***    ***      *   *****   ***   *****   ***    ***    *** 
*  *   *  *   *    **   *      *          *  *   *  *   *  *   *
*    **      *    * *   ****   ****      *    ***    ****  *   *
*   *     *   *  *****      *  *   *    *    *   *      *  *   *
*  *****   ***      *   ****    ***    *      ***    ***    *** 
```
